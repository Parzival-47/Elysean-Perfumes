Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who We Are

Elysean Perfumes is a luxury fragrance house based in South Africa. We sell handcrafted Eau de Parfum online through our website at elyseanperfumes.co.za. When you interact with our website or place an order, we collect and use certain personal information. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

For any questions about this policy, contact us at elyseanperfumes@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when placing an order:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Order details including products, sizes, and quantities

We do not collect or store any payment card information. All payments are processed securely by Yoco, our third-party payment provider.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Process and fulfill your order
  • Send you an order confirmation email with your receipt
  • Contact you about your delivery if needed
  • Respond to any questions or queries you send us

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Payment Processing

All payments on our website are processed by Yoco, a South African payment service provider. When you complete a purchase, you are redirected to Yoco's secure hosted checkout page. We do not have access to your card details at any point. Yoco's own privacy policy governs how they handle your payment information.

5. Email Communications

When you place an order, we send you a transactional confirmation email containing your order details and delivery address. This email is sent via Brevo, our email service provider. We do not send unsolicited marketing emails. If you contact us directly, we will use your email address only to respond to your query.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your order information is stored temporarily on our secure server to process your order and send your confirmation email. We take reasonable technical precautions to protect your data. We do not retain your personal information longer than necessary to fulfill your order.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent to any processing based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at elyseanperfumes@gmail.com and we will respond within a reasonable time.

8. Cookies

Our website uses your browser's local storage to save your cart items between visits. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics at this time.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
