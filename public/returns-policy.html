Returns Policy

Last updated: June 2026

We want you to love every fragrance you receive from Elysean Perfumes. If something is not right with your order, we will do everything we can to make it right. Please read this policy carefully before placing your order.

1. Our Commitment

At Elysean Perfumes, every bottle is carefully inspected and packaged before dispatch. We take pride in the quality and integrity of every order we send out. In the rare event that something goes wrong, we stand behind our products and our customers.

2. Returns on Sealed Products

Due to the nature of fragrance products, we are unable to accept returns on opened or used bottles for hygiene and safety reasons. However if you receive a product that is:

  • Damaged or broken during delivery
  • Leaking or defective
  • Incorrect — meaning you received the wrong product

We will offer you a full replacement or refund at no additional cost to you. Please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order.

3. Sample Bottles

We offer 2ml sample sizes specifically so you can try a fragrance before committing to a full bottle. We strongly recommend ordering a sample first if you are unsure about a scent. For this reason, returns on full-size bottles opened after delivery are not accepted, as the sample option was available prior to purchase.

4. How to Request a Return or Replacement

To request a return or replacement, contact us within 7 days of receiving your order with the following:

  • Your full name and order number
  • A clear photograph of the damaged or incorrect product
  • A brief description of the issue

Send this information to elyseanperfumes@gmail.com or WhatsApp us on 064 857 0979. We will respond within 2 business days.

5. Refunds

If your return or replacement request is approved, refunds will be processed back to your original payment method within 5 to 10 business days depending on your bank. We will confirm the refund via email once it has been processed on our side.

Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error, such as a wrong or damaged product.

6. Delivery and Shipping

We currently deliver within South Africa only. Standard delivery takes 3 to 7 business days depending on your location. A flat shipping fee of R50 applies to all orders. Once your order has been dispatched you will be notified.

Please ensure your delivery address is entered correctly at checkout. Elysean Perfumes cannot be held responsible for orders delivered to an incorrect address provided by the customer.

7. Cancellations

If you need to cancel your order, please contact us immediately at elyseanperfumes@gmail.com. We will do our best to cancel the order before it is dispatched. Once an order has been dispatched, it cannot be cancelled and the standard returns process applies.

8. Contact Us

For any questions about returns, refunds, or your order, please reach out to us directly:

  • Email: elyseanperfumes@gmail.com
  • Phone / WhatsApp: 064 857 0979
  • Website: elyseanperfumes.co.za
